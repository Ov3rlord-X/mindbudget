MindBudget

A personal companion for mental wellness and financial mindfulness — built specifically for Nigerian university students.

What is MindBudget?

MindBudget is a student wellness app that recognises something most apps ignore — that how you feel and how you spend your money are deeply connected. A student who runs out of money in the second week of the month is not just broke, they are anxious, distracted, and often too ashamed to ask for help.

MindBudget gives you one place to take care of both — your mind and your money.

📓

💸

🌙

Built For Nigerian Students

MindBudget was designed with the Nigerian university experience in mind — limited allowances, academic pressure, the struggle to find affordable mental health support, and the reality of inconsistent internet access. Everything about this app is built around your actual daily life.
